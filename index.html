Youtube Search

Search results default to being sorted by Relevance. Other valid sorting options:
Relevance: Resources are sorted based on their relevance to the search query. This is the default value for this parameter.
Date: Resources are sorted in reverse chronological order based on the date they were created.
Rating: Resources are sorted from highest to lowest rating.
Title: Resources are sorted alphabetically by title.
Video Count: Channels are sorted in descending order of their number of uploaded videos.
View Count: Resources are sorted from highest to lowest number of views. For live broadcasts, videos are sorted by number of concurrent viewers while the broadcasts are ongoing.
